I'm

Multi-Disciplinary Designer, Artist, Explorer, Photographer

As a Multi-disciplinary designer, I breathe life into projects with boundless exploration and a vibrant touch.

During my time at NMIMS School of Design, I've embraced a multifaceted journey into various design domains, including UI/UX, product design, communication design, graphics, ethnography, and photography. This diverse creative palette allows me to blend artistry, functionality, and cultural insights, creating a holistic design experience that continually inspires and challenges me to grow. Design, to me, is not just about aesthetics but also about crafting meaningful user-centered experiences that tell a story, and I'm eager to apply this philosophy as I continue to evolve and shape the future of design.

UX DESIGN

I relish UX design for its thrilling blend of psychology, creativity, and problem-solving, turning user experiences into delightful journeys.

BRAND IDENTITY

Brand identity is my playground of imagination, where I craft visual stories that resonate, connecting people with brands in meaningful and memorable ways.

UI DESIGN

UI design is where I turn function into art, creating digital magic with style and substance.

PRODUCT DESIGN

Product design is my passion, where I transform ideas into tangible solutions that improve lives, one innovation at a time.

DATA VISUALISATION

Data visualization is my tool to turn complexity into clarity, making numbers dance and stories emerge.

ETHNOGRAPHY

PHOTOGRAPHY
